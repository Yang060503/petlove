About PetLove

At PetLove, we believe that pets are family. Founded in 2025, our mission has been to provide pet owners with the highest quality products and the most convenient shopping experience possible.

Our Story

PetLove began when our founder, a passionate pet owner, struggled to find high-quality pet food and accessories in one convenient location. Frustrated by the limited options available, the idea for PetLove was born - a one-stop shop for all pet needs, delivered with love and care.

What started as a small local business has grown into Malaysia's most trusted online pet store, serving thousands of happy pets and their owners nationwide.

Our Promise

We carefully select every product in our store to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, safety, and nutritional value. All our products are:

  • Sourced from trusted brands and manufacturers
  • Rigorously tested for quality and safety
  • Nutritionally balanced for pet health
  • Environmentally responsible whenever possible
